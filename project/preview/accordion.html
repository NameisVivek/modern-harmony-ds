How do I create a new scenario? add

Go to S&OP Planning, click "New scenario", select a base forecast and adjust the parameters.

What does the forecast accuracy metric show? add

Forecast accuracy compares the predicted demand against actual demand for the previous period, expressed as a percentage.

Can I export forecast data? add

Yes — use the Export button in the toolbar. Choose CSV or Excel. Large exports may take a few minutes.

How are high-risk items identified? add

Items exceeding the delay or deviation threshold defined in Configuration are automatically flagged as high-risk.
